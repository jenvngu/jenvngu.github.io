Intro

I am currently working as a Data Scientist and contribute to the execution of SBIR contracts and work with multiple agencies within the DoD. My expertise is in research, development, data visualization and information storytelling, technical documentation, as well as facilitating customer outreach.

I have a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics from Kennesaw State University. Within the KSU College of Computing and Software Engineering, I’ve worked as a Research Assistant to research and develop products/solutions utilizing computer vision for healthcare applications.

Prior to working as a Data Scientist, I’ve worked in software development and supported the development of websites to demonstrate API products to potential customers, the design of financial metrics dashboards, and deep learning research and data exploration in applying natural language processing to customer text messages.

Parallel to my work in research and development in machine learning, I also contribute to creating custom dashboards and user interfaces, performing technical research, composing reports and proposals, implementing custom data solutions, and teambuilding.

As I continue to progress in my career, I am actively working to advance my engineering and mathematical interpretation skills. I strive to take on additional leadership roles to aid others in. With each new project, I enjoy contributing to the continuous process improvement for product development and general business practices.

When I'm not coding or analyzing data, you can find me exploring the great outdoors, painting on a canvas, or hopping on the next Delta flight.
